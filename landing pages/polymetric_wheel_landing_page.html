Benefits

Useful at the exact moment a loop gets interesting.

Polymetric Wheel turns timing data into a visual surface producers can scan fast.

01

Private by design

Your Ableton project is parsed locally in the browser, so ideas stay on your machine.

02

Rhythm first

Each ring is a track, each dot is a hit or note onset, and the loop start is always clear.

03

Mix-aware playback

Add a wav or mp3 bounce and the wheel follows your real audio instead of a placeholder synth.

How It Works

From project file to moving map in three steps.

1

Drop the project

Load an Ableton .als or XML file. Add a bounce at the same time when you want real mix playback.

2

Read the structure

The parser extracts tempo, meter, loop length, tracks, onsets, and sidechain timing cues.

3

Play the wheel

The playhead, dot flashes, and audio stay locked to the same transport through every loop.
